Grilled Peaches & Cream

Description

These grilled peaches & cream are a great way to use up those peaches from the orchard. They are filled with a mixture of cream cheese, honey, and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 4 peaches, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons clover honey
  • 1 cup soft cream cheese with honey and nuts
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

Step Instruction
1 Preheat a grill for medium-high heat.
2 Brush peaches with a light coating of oil. Place pit side down onto the grill. Grill for 5 minutes, or until the surfaces have nice grill marks.
3 Turn the peaches over, and drizzle with a bit of honey. Place a dollop of the cream cheese spread in the place where the pit was.
4 Grill for 2 to 3 more minutes, or until the filling is warm. Serve immediately.
